# NovaRune Staking Mechanics

## Overview
Staking NovaRune is the primary way to participate in securing and operating the xMesh / NovaNet infrastructure.

## Staking Process
1. Lock NovaRune to a node identity (operator key)
2. Register the node on the mesh (via Nexus or on-chain registration)
3. Earn rewards based on uptime, bandwidth contribution, and peer quality

## Slashing Conditions
- Extended downtime
- Malicious routing or data tampering
- Failure to complete assigned relay tasks

## Unbonding Period
- 21 days (subject to Nexus governance adjustments)

## Nexus Role
Nexus monitors node performance in real time and can adjust effective staking weight or trigger partial slashing based on objective metrics.
